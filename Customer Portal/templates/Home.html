How to reset your password

Back to Knowledge Base
Category: Account & Security
Last updated: October 2, 2023

Steps to reset your password

If you've forgotten your password or need to reset it for security reasons, follow these steps:

1. Go to the password reset page

Click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page or visit https://example.com/reset-password directly.

2. Enter your email address

Input the email address associated with your account and click "Send Reset Link".

Note:

If you don't receive the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder. Some email providers may filter automated messages.

3. Check your email

You'll receive an email with a password reset link. Click on this link to proceed.

Security Tip:

Reset links expire after 24 hours for security reasons. If your link has expired, you'll need to request a new one.

4. Create a new password

On the password reset page, enter your new password twice to confirm.

Your password must:

  • Be at least 8 characters long
  • Contain at least one uppercase letter
  • Contain at least one number
  • Contain at least one special character (!@#$%^&*)

5. Log in with your new password

After successfully resetting your password, you'll be redirected to the login page where you can sign in with your new credentials.

Troubleshooting

Not receiving the reset email?

If you're not receiving the password reset email, try the following:

  • Check your spam or junk mail folder
  • Add no-reply@example.com to your email contacts
  • Wait a few minutes - some email providers delay automated messages
  • Ensure you're checking the email associated with your account

If you still don't receive the email, contact our support team for assistance.

Reset link not working?

Password reset links expire after 24 hours. If your link isn't working:

  • Request a new password reset
  • Ensure you're clicking the link in the most recent email
  • Try copying and pasting the link directly into your browser
